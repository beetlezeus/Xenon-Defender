# Xenon Defender

Xenon Defender is an arcade-style z-axis rail shooter that puts you in control of a starship, defending the planet Xenon from waves of enemy invaders.
With fast-paced action, intuitive controls, and a focus on precision shooting, Xenon Defender challenges you to master the art of survival while racking up massive scores.
